My Intuition Made Me Do It

Author A. K. Bird

Women's Fiction Roman a Clef

A story of 'Girl Finds Self', this tale takes a reader deep into the experience of finding one's higher calling and overcoming the challenge of loving and letting go.

This Journey will resonate with those who are finding their way to the finish line of a dead-end relationship. For those who are uncovering their calling and purpose. For those who are interested in Spirituality and believe that our reality can offer us more than our imaginations could dream of for ourselves.
